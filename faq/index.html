Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our boosting services. Can't find your answer? Join our Discord.

How do I place an order?
You place an order by opening a ticket on our Discord server. Our team will guide you through the entire process from there.

What games do you boost?
We boost Rocket League, Fortnite, Valorant, and Rainbow Six Siege. Each game has its own pricing calculator on our homepage. We're always looking to add more games, join our Discord for announcements.
Is boosting safe? Will I get banned?
Boosting is completely safe. We have a 0% ban rate across thousands of completed boosts across all games. Your account is in good hands.
How does the boosting process work?
Depending on your chosen method, we either log onto your account (Solo) or play together with you (Duo) until you reach your desired rank. This applies to all games we offer.
What is the difference between Solo and Duo?
Solo means we play on your account, it's quicker, cheaper, and overall the better option. Duo means we play together with you, it takes longer, costs 50% more, and is only available on weekends. Duo is available for Rocket League, Fortnite, Valorant, and R6 Siege.
How long does a boost take?
It depends on the game and rank range. Rocket League boosts typically take 1–2 days. Fortnite and R6 Siege vary based on rank distance. Valorant Immortal+ boosts can take up to 2 weeks. Use our calculator for an estimated time.
Do you need my account login?
Yes, for Solo boosts we will need your account login information. Your credentials are kept secure and only used for the duration of the boost. We recommend changing your password after the boost is completed.
Does my region affect the price?
For Valorant and R6 Siege, NA is our base price. Any other region (EU, OCE, LATAM, APAC, KR, BR) costs 50% more due to higher queue times and ping requirements. If you're also selecting Duo, the multipliers add up, so Duo on a non-NA region would be +100% total.
What is Fortnite PR Boosting?
PR (Power Ranking) Boosting is where we earn PR points on your Fortnite account. It costs $0.10 per PR point and is Solo only. Your account must have 14/14 events played, 2FA enabled, and be eligible for prize money tournaments.
What Rocket League playlists do you boost?
We boost all playlists: 1v1, 2v2, 3v3, Rumble, Hoops, Snow Day, and Heatseeker. We also do tournament boosts, season rewards, and custom orders.
Do you boost on all platforms?
Yes, we boost on all platforms - PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch. For Valorant, we boost on PC only (the only platform it's available on).
Can I play on my account while being boosted?
No, you cannot play on your account during a Solo boost. Our booster needs uninterrupted access to complete the order efficiently and safely. For Duo boosts, you'll be playing together with us.
